Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: 29 June 2026

SkillForge ("we", "us", or "our") operates this website and our learning platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our site or enroll in our programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details you provide — name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you enroll.
  • Usage data — pages visited, courses viewed, and time spent on the platform.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our courses and services.
  • Process enrollments and payments.
  • Send updates, learning materials, and important notices.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how the site is used. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as payment processors and email tools) strictly to operate our services, and only when required by law.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

For any privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@skillforge.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
