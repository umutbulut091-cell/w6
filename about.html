We're on a mission to make skills accessible

SkillForge was built on one belief — that anyone, anywhere, can learn the skills to change their life with the right guidance and real practice.

Our story

From a small study group to a global community

What started in 2018 as a weekend study group of five friends teaching each other to code has grown into a worldwide bootcamp with thousands of graduates across 40+ countries.

We saw that traditional education was too slow, too theoretical, and too expensive. So we built a different model — hands-on, mentor-led, and laser-focused on getting people hired.

  • Built by industry practitioners, not just academics
  • Every course ends with a real, portfolio-ready project
  • Dedicated career team until you land a role
